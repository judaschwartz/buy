Boss Capital Review

Boss Capital welcomes binary option traders from all over the globe including the US! Expect the same great features present on sister sites, Redwood Options and TradeRush. Boss Capital provides an easy-to-use interface for trading over 100 different types of assets with returns of up to 85%.

Rating

Traders at Boss Capital have an excellent range of 100 + assets to trade with Call/Put, Short Term, Boundary, or Touch trading. The site is free of bells and whistles and allows the user to focus on the reason he or she came there in the first place- to complete a trade. Unlike many binary options brokers online, Boss Capital offers it services to US traders as well.

Despite that fact that Boss Capital is a relatively new broker, the wide variety of assets including almost 30 indices belies its freshness in the industry. The site is powered by TechFinancials, and traders will find commodities, currencies and stocks, as well as numerous ways to trade (High/Low, Touch, No Touch, Boundary, Pairs, 30- or 60-Second trades) for both desktop and mobile platforms.

The customer support at Boss Capital is excellent and available through different local numbers, live chat, and email. Boss Capital accepts major credit cards and most e-wallet payment methods in a variety of currencies including US Dollar, Euro, British Pound, Japanese Yen, Australian Dollar, Canadian Dollar and Russian Rubles. The minimum deposit is 200 of the selected currency, and there is no minimum withdrawal requirement.

While the welcome bonus only makes it up to 50%, there are other perks including further deposit bonuses, risk-free binary options trades, cash back deals and more. VIP accounts also benefit from special insurance plans.

It is exciting to see a broker that opens the door to US traders with a standard if healthy return rate of 85%! Boss Capital offers a great variety of assets and options which creates a diverse trading experience and a wide selection of educational materials for all skill levels.
