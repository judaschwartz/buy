24option.com Review

For the trader looking to work with one of the industry's best known names, 24option provides excellent educational resources, great customer support, and free trading signals. Trade on the world's top brands including the recently added Facebook, Amex and Disney, and look forward to return rates that hit the 89% mark!

Rating

24option's sophisticated site is accessible in 15 different languages on both desktop and mobile platforms. 24option offers up plenty of background information for free in the form of e-books, a series of e-courses, and all the on-site materials in between. Traders can choose from indices, currencies, commodities, and stocks and will find all the necessary details to master trading definitions, hours, and pairings.

The required deposit of $250 includes access to the 24option demo account that enables you to test drive your strategies before taking them to market. While the platform is largely intuitive and easy-to-use, 24option didn't skimp on the amount of information displayed in their interface, making it one of the more information-heavy platforms available to traders. 24option traders can cancel the trade up to five minutes before expiry in order to not lose on the entire trade.

24option requires a $250 minimum deposit, aligning itself with the industry standard on this front. The site is offered in a total of 15 languages along with a customer support team that can be reached via phone, email, or chat. Accepts all major credit cards, e-wallets, and wire transfers

While 24option is generally recognized as one of the superior trading platforms, the bonuses are not among the industry's largest. Still, the benefits of the interface and exceptional resources outweigh the lighter cash incentives.

Registration is fast and easy, and the minimum deposit comes with the highly recommend demo account. An agreed upon industry favorite (for users outside the US), 24option is a great place to start or continue developing your portfolio.
