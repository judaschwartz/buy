Regal Options Review

With 150 assets for traders to choose from and a variety of trading options, Regal Options lives up to its name. The 100% welcome bonus and a highly navigable platform are draws for traders of all skill levels as well as the extra security of being operated on the CySEC-regulated TradoLogic platform.

Rating

Regal Options has been on the binary options scene since 2013 and is powered by TradoLogic , an EU supervised trading platform. Traders can register in a few steps, open a contract, and select the details of the trade without any prior experience. The site makes the special offers, education, and other required materials visible and understandable even for beginning traders.

The TradoLogic platform contains the standard call/put, digital binary options, high yield touch, and range options. The broker presents a wide range of assets that includes all the major indices from around the world, over a dozen commodities including coffee and sugar, 13 major forex pairs and over 100 stocks. One Touch and Range Options can yield higher payouts, and the standard payout options fall in the range of 75-81%.

Regal OPtions offers 24/7 LiveChat and phone support in all of the three supported languages on the site. The list of available payment options is relatively short and includes major credit card, wire transfer, NETELLER, and Cashu. While the list is short, the details, requirements, and transfer times of each are very clearly laid out in the site’s Banking page.

Open an account at RegalOptions, and enjoy a 100% welcome bonus! Further bonuses may be available from an account manager, and the bonus amount grows alongside your deposits. It is important to note that the trading minimum is 30x the bonus and may be subject to time constraints as well.

Regal Options’ platform will appeal to traders of all stripes. The platform is easy to use and intuitive and  offers a wide variety of assets and the incentives to make it worthwhile. While the site is still working on expanding its language offerings, the site is regulated by the EU’s CySEC body which means that additional languages can’t be far off!
