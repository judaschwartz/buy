FMTrader Review

FMTrader is a recent addition to the binary market place built on SpotOption's white label, web-based trading platform and run by Broad Media Ltd. FMTrader presents a clean, easy-to-follow, and information-packed site ideal for the new trader looking to escape promotional overload and earn returns of up to 90%!

Rating

Traders at FMTrader don't need to download software and experience little chance of getting lost in a sea of information that often confuses the homepage of many brokers. The interface strikes the right balance between remaining user-friendly and delivering all the information that users truly need. A standard range of currencies, indices, stocks, and commodities are available for trading; binary options can yield up to a 90% profit while one touch options can soar as high as 550%!

Trading connoisseurs may recognize some of the features of the Spot Option platform in FMTrader's site. The registration is short, there is no software download, and binary options, options builder, one touch, and 60 second trades are standard features.

FMTrader customer support is active around the clock by email, phone, or chat. In order to deposit or withdraw money, traders are required to submit documentation to authenticate the account. Visa Electron, Maestro, CashU, wire transfer, debit cards, and credit cards are all welcome.

FMTrader offers a wide variety of bonuses for first-time traders and referrals. Accounts have a $10,000 per year general bonus cap and a $5,000 per year referral bonus cap. For new traders, the bonus will correspond to the amount of your initial deposit.

While FMTrader is competing with veterans, its advantages outweigh its green status: no software to download, a free demo account, a wide range of currency pairs, commodities, stocks, and indices, binary options, option builder, one touch, and 60 seconds. The platform is intuitive, easy-to-use and customer support is highly responsive. As FMTrader expands it operation, we will surely see it join the ranks of the industry's top names!
